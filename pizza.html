Description

Pizza is a very good food if you need something fast to order or even if you have some ingredients in your fridge. It is very fun to make pizza with family/friends and to compare your result with others.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepperoni
  • Salami
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes sauce
  • Whatever you want

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water and salt until dough is formed
  2. Let the dough to grow up
  3. Prepare the pan with the dough well covered and let it grow up too
  4. Add the ingredients you want
  5. Introduce pan into the oven and let it cook at low fire
