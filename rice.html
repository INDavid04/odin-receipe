Description

Rice can become very complicated to prepare if you want that the rice do not get sticky, and to see each little piece of rice. It is very good to eat instead of bread. For example, you can eat rice with chicken, fish and others.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Boil the rice
  2. Cook the onion first, then carrot
  3. Add all the ingredients
  4. Add salt and pepper by taste
  5. Cook the meat and voila
