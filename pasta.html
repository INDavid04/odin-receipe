Description

Pasta is an italian food. It can be many type, for example: spaghetty, panne, paprika. This is just three examples of the multitude of pasta in the whole world.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sour cream
  • Pork loin
  • Egg yolk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cut the pork loin and fry
  3. Add egg yolk in the sour cream
  4. When pasta is almost cooked, add the rest of ingredients
  5. Add salt, pepper and others by your taste
